DATA ANALYST

Trained as a financial analyst as i studied I'm a data analyst with experience in multiple data tools and technologies, including Microsoft Excel, business intelligence, SQL, Python, statistics, Power-Bi and Tableau. My focus is on leveraging and wrangling data to drive business value and solve real-world problems.

Services

Data Analysis

I have used Python to analyze customer data and segment customers based on their demographics, behaviors, and preferences. I have also built interactive visualizations in Tableau and Power-Bi to present the customer segments and their corresponding characteristics. I provide recommendations to the marketing team to improve customer targeting and retention.

Dashboards

I create dashboards to show the most recent trends and results in an interactive way.

Advice and Coaching Projects

I give advice to other data analysts and coach them when working on data related projects. I give updates before and during a project.

(Interactive)Infographics

I create interactive infographics which can be used publicly and internally. I can help you present your data in an inspiring way rather than through text.
